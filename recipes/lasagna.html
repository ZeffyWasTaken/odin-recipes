Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish comprised of layers of wide pasta sheets, rich tomato sauce, savory ground meat, creamy béchamel, and gooey melted cheese, baked to perfection.

Its indulgent layers of flavors and textures create a comforting and hearty meal loved for its satisfyingly delicious taste.

Ingredients

  • Wide pasta sheets
  • Tomato sauce (with herbs like basil and oregano)
  • Ground meat (beef, pork, sausage)
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Eggs (for binding)
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Optional: Vegetables like spinach or mushrooms

Steps

  1. Make the tomato sauce with onions, garlic, herbs, and meat.
  2. Mix ricotta, Parmesan, eggs, salt, and pepper for the cheese layer.
  3. Layer sauce, pasta, cheese mixture, and mozzarella in a baking dish.
  4. Cover with foil and bake at 350°F (180°C) for 25-30 mins, then uncover and bake for another 10-15 mins until golden.
  5. Let it rest for 10-15 minutes before slicing and serving. Enjoy your lasagna!
